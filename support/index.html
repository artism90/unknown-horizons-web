Support

Hello, if you have a problem with Unknown Horizons you are in the correct place to get help and solve your problem.

Before continuing with further steps, please see if your problem is already answered in our problem database (FAQ)!

If your problem is not answered in the problem database, you have a few options:

Get in contact with the team directly using our IRC Chat (also webbased).

Leave us a message on the Forum, useful if no one is available on IRC.

Leave a bugreport on our Bug Tracker at Github.
